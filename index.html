About Us

Guiding souls through authentic teachings of the Qur’an and Sunnah, fostering a generation rooted in knowledge and piety.

Our Mission

To carry on the business of establishing, managing, and operating Islamiyya schools and comprehensive educational institutions that provide high-quality learning in both Islamic and Western education. This includes the provision of structured academic programs at all levels, namely pre-nursery, nursery, primary, and secondary education, designed to promote intellectual growth, moral discipline, and spiritual development.

The company aims to deliver sound Islamic education based on the teachings of the Qur’an and Sunnah alongside a standard Western curriculum, ensuring a balanced and holistic educational system that prepares students for both religious and worldly success.

The company may also establish and operate special schools and learning centers tailored to meet the needs of individuals requiring specialized education, including children with unique learning needs.

In addition, the organization shall provide consultancy services in the field of education, including but not limited to school establishment, accreditation guidance, curriculum planning, educational management, staff training, and institutional development, both within Nigeria and internationally.

Our Educational Programs

Tailored courses for all ages — from foundational Qur'anic literacy to advanced Islamic sciences.

Tajweed & Recitation

Master the rules of Tajweed with certified Qaris. One-on-one and group sessions to beautify your Quran recitation.

Hifz (Memorization)

Structured Hifz program with daily revision (Sabaq, Sabaqi, Daur). Individual progress tracking and ijazah preparation.

Seerah & Sunnah Studies

In-depth courses on prophetic biography, Fiqh, and Hadith sciences for adults and youth — based on reliable scholarship.

Children's Islamic Program

Fun & interactive learning: Arabic alphabets, short Surahs, daily Duas, and Islamic manners (Adab).

Arabic Language

From beginner to advanced — conversational and classical Arabic to understand the Quran directly.

Weekend & Online Classes

Flexible schedules for working professionals & distant students. Live online classes with recorded backup.
